About Us
Compete
We organize large-scale events where founders compete for funding. Winners automatically get access to the NeXidea ecosystem, including advisors, media, experts, resources, incubators, and accelerators.
Invest
We invest in pre-seed and seed startups. We source startups from our existing incubator and accelerator network. In addition, we conduct thorough due diligence with our well-rounded team of analysts.
Scale
We work closely with our portfolio companies to help them scale and acheive their full potential, leveraging our extensive worldwide network to provide additional funding, strategic partnerships, and customers.
Investment Thesis
We focus on startups that are leveraging technologies to solve real-world problems, with a preference for companies founded by diverse teams that have a strong social impact. We invest in companies at various stages of development, with a particular emphasis on seed and early-stage startups.
